Computer Bids

Link to base html page: Computer Bid Template Page
Oraganize a price list for two custom computers.
The prices will be posted on your girhub.com site.
One computer will be below $1000 and the other compouter
will be under $2500.
Document the price of each item and where it was purchased.
Save this bid in a file name computerbid.html and upload it to
your usename.github.io website.
Here is an example: https://craigcoleman.github.io
Make  sure to include the name of the part, the price, and where
it was purchased.
PCPartpicker is an excellent resource: https://pcpartpicker.com/
